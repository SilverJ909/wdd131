Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is for those needing help finding a Japanese Kanji kana and or making sentence with Japanese kana

Audience

Japanese learners or those who just need a refresher on Kanji or writing a sentence - It's assumed they understand Japanese sentence structure, and mainly

Dynamic elements

Mainly, using a search bar to find and display the kanji related to the string typed in the serch bar - whether it's related to the English or Romanji. There will be a big text box where they can type, but is mainly used for making sentences with Japanese Kana

Pseudocode for your project

Search Function: In the event you press the search button, if the input's value string includes either the English or the Romanji (KUN or ON reading) of a character, then the render card function will run Render card: a character card will show up displaying a picture of the Kanji, the English meaning (by itself), the ON and KUN Furigana (the Hirigana-another Japanese writing system- of the Kanji), the Romanji (the romanized spelling of the Japanese Kana), and a button to call a function that writes the character on another input box.
